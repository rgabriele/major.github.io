Leamington's Best Buildings

With so many architectural marvels in one town, it's hard to pick just one structure that stands above the rest. So here we have compiled a list of only the best buildings Leamington has to offer.

Leamington's best buildings are:

  • The Water Tower
  • Nature Fresh Farms Recreation Centre
  • The Big Tomato
  • Town Hall

The Water Tower

Seen as a landmark of the town for decades, the red (tomato) water tower overlooks many of the greenhouses along the lake. It serves as a reminder of the importance of irrigation in agriculture.

Nature Fresh Farms Recreation Centre

Originally opened in 1985 as the F.T. Sherk Aquatic and Fitness Centre, the Nature Fresh Farms Recreation Centre is the hub for all of Leamington's sports events. Home of the Leamington Flyers, the centre holds 2 ice pads - the main rink Highbury Canco Arena and the smaller Unico Arena.

The Big Tomato

One of Leamington's most iconic structures, the big tomato is a staple of the downtown core. The tomato serves as a tourist information center in the summer to inform visitors of the many attractions the town has to offer.

Town Hall

Built in recent years on taxpayers' money, Leamington's town hall boasts a modern and chic architecture surely to make surrounding towns jealous.
