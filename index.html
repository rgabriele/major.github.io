Welcome to Leamington

The most exhilarating town in the world

Originally incorporated as a village in 1874, Leamington has blossomed into the bustling metropolis it is today. Being the second largest municipality in the Windsor-Essex County area (after the city of Windsor), the town boasts a diverse community. Although defined by its agricultural heritage, Leamington has something to offer for everyone; whether it be the exciting nightlife, michelin star restaurants, or the enchanting Point Pelee National Park, there is always something to do in this town.

This site was made to show you the best things Leamington has to offer!
