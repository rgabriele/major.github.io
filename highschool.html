Leamington's Best High School

Although it may look like a prison on the outside, Leamington District Secondary School feels like a home on the inside. This place is where I made some of my fondest teenage memories. Like when I juggled in the school talent show to Evanescence's hit track Bring Me to Life and became the most popular guy in school. Or that time Dequavious made me eat the moldy fry in the cafeteria or else he'd give me a wedgie. Good times.
